An ongoing exploration into building a lean e-commerce operation through TikTok Shop and Carousell, focused on the Singapore market.

## The Thesis

Short-form video commerce is growing fast in Southeast Asia, and there's a window to build a small but profitable operation by identifying underserved product niches and pairing them with content-driven distribution.

## Current Focus Areas

Two product categories are in active exploration: home cleaning products (high repeat purchase, low brand loyalty, strong demo potential on video) and a luxury incense brand (higher margin, aesthetic-driven, suited for lifestyle content).

## What I'm Learning

Product sourcing, margin analysis, content creation for commerce, and the operational mechanics of running a TikTok Shop in Singapore. The goal isn't to build a massive brand - it's to understand the full stack of modern e-commerce from the inside.
