A B2B brokerage model designed to bridge the gap between FMCG/pharma companies seeking sustainable packaging solutions and the fragmented supplier landscape in Southeast Asia.

## The Opportunity

Large FMCG and pharmaceutical companies face increasing regulatory and consumer pressure to adopt sustainable packaging, but struggle to navigate the fragmented supplier ecosystem — especially in ASEAN markets where material certifications, minimum order quantities, and supply reliability vary wildly.

## The Approach

Using domain knowledge from chemical engineering (material properties, barrier performance, shelf-life implications) and product management experience (stakeholder coordination, go-to-market), the brokerage model provides spec-matching, supplier vetting, and ongoing quality oversight as a service layer.

## Current Status

Playbook developed for Singapore market launch. Validating demand through conversations with mid-tier FMCG brands and exploring supplier partnerships in Malaysia, Thailand, and Indonesia.
