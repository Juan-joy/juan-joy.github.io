Co-founder

Light Patches

What if you could take care of your oral health while you sleep? Light Patches is an overnight oral care film that sticks to your gum and delivers active ingredients over 8 hours through a bio-dissolvable bilayer system. You wake up without bad breath, with a cleaner, healthier mouth. No rinsing. No routine. Just peel, stick, and sleep.

NUS VIP Grant Winner co-founder oral care HPMC films biotech

01

The Problem

Traditional oral care products work for seconds or minutes. We asked: why not use the 8 hours you spend sleeping?

02

The Science

Bilayer HPMC film: one layer for mucoadhesion, one for sustained release of active ingredients overnight

03

Where We Are

Prototype 3 complete. Winners of the NUS Venture Initiation Programme grant. Currently sourcing GMP-grade materials

04

What's Next

Pilot batch production, user testing, and exploring regulatory pathways in Singapore and ASEAN

My Role

Co-founder, Product & R&D

I co-founded Light Patches to apply my chemical engineering background to a real consumer problem. My work spans formulation R&D (designing the bilayer film system, optimising plasticizer ratios and release kinetics), product strategy (positioning, go-to-market planning), and fundraising (NUS VIP Grant pitch and application). This project combines the technical rigour of my ChemE training with the product instincts I developed at Shopee, AAX Biotech, and Standard Pharma.
